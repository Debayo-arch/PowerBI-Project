# PowerBI-Project
## [Project: Maven Eectronics Sales Dashboard](https://github.com/Debayo-arch/PowerBI-Project)

## Project Overview
This Power BI project analyzes the sales performance of a Maven electronics retailer operating across multiple regions, product categories, and customer demographics.
The interactive dashboard provides a unified view of revenue, profit, product trends, customer behavior, and store performance between **2016 and 2021.**
The goal of this analysis is to help management understand **key drivers of performance**, identify **underperforming areas**, and make **data-driven decisions** for revenue growth and customer retention.

## Tools used 
- Power BI Desktop
- Power Query
- DAX

## Key Business Questions Answered
This report provides insights into:
1. Overall Sales Performance
- Total Revenue, Profit, Quantity Sold, and Customer Count
- Year-over-Year (YoY) performance
- Month-over-Month (MoM) growth trends

2. Product Performance
- Best-selling and underperforming products
- Top 10 vs Bottom 10 product categories & subcategories
- Profit margins and cost trends

3. Customer Insights
- Demographic performance (Age groups, Gender, Geography)
- New vs Returning customers
- Revenue contribution by customer segments

4. Store & Regional Performance
- Regional revenue trends
- High-performing vs declining states
- Store performance based on opening date (New vs Old stores)

## Insights & Recommendations (Summary)
1. Revenue peaked between 2016–2020 but declined sharply in 2021. Especially in the United States, which contributed most to the drop.
2. Computers are the most profitable categories
3. Games & Toys contribute the least to revenue and profit.
4. The Elders generate the highest revenue
5. The Young Adult contribute the least — marketing efforts can target this group.
6. Older stores outperform new stores significantly
7. New stores may need stronger marketing or better customer acquisition strategies.
8. Certain regions are driving the overall decline. Targeted campaigns should be launched in underperforming regions, especially France.
